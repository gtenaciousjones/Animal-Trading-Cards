Boxer

"Man's best friend," the domestic dog, and humans have had one of the longest relationships in history, spanning thousands of years.

  • Scientific name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average length: 2.5 - 42 in. (at shoulder)
  • Average lifespan: 10 - 13 years
  • Habitat: Wherever humans live

The global dog population is an estimated 900 million, with over 339 breeds. The breeds vary significantly in size. Some smaller breeds, such as the Russkiy Toy, weigh only 3 lbs, while giant breeds, such as the English Mastif, weigh as much as 343 lbs!
